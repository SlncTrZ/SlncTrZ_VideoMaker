# Documentary & Expository · Director's Non-Fiction Technique Skill Pack

## Introduction

This skill pack provides a comprehensive set of director's non-fiction technique references for the **Documentary & Expository** genre, covering the full workflow from macro-level narrative planning to micro-level storyboard execution. Applicable to any visual style.

## Core Philosophy

- **Truth First** — Every creative decision serves the truth of the subject. Authenticity and factual integrity are non-negotiable foundations of the documentary approach
- **Clarity is King** — Information must be communicated with precision and economy. The audience should never feel lost or confused about what they are seeing and why
- **Structure Drives Understanding** — A well-structured argument is more powerful than any single image. Logical progression — not emotional manipulation — is the primary storytelling engine
- **Visuals Serve Information** — Every visual choice (composition, motion, graphic) exists to illuminate the subject, not to decorate it. Form follows function in documentary craft

## Skill File Description

### 1. Narrative Planning Techniques (`director_planning_narrative.md`)

Technique references used by directors during the **narrative planning phase**, defining overall planning methods for Documentary & Expository in thematic focus, information rhythm, evidence design, and sound direction.

| Section | Content Summary |
|---|---|
| Thematic Focus & Information Core | Objective truth anchor, thesis-first approach, evidence hierarchy, logical chain, neutrality, data visualization |
| Narrative Structure & Rhythm Planning | Problem-Solution, Chronological Timeline, Compare-Contrast, Cause-Effect, Pyramid/Inverted — with info density curve |
| Scene-by-Scene Evidence Design | Hook/Opening, Data Visualization, Expert Interview, Case Study, Timeline Progression, Conclusion/Call to Action |
| Sound & Music Direction | Instrument selection for credibility vs emotional weight, environmental sounds for documentary realism |
| Composition & Visual Style | 2.5D parallax techniques, kinetic typography, data visualization styles, archival footage treatment |
| Camera Movement & Rhythm | Controlled motion, slow push/pull for emphasis, parallax depth transitions, static-to-dynamic pacing |

### 2. Storyboard Table Narrative Techniques (`director_storyboard_table_narrative.md`)

Technique references used by directors during the **storyboard table creation phase**, defining Documentary & Expository in shot-size information mapping, camera vitality, timing control, shot merging, and transition logic.

| Section | Content Summary |
|---|---|
| Storyboard Table Positioning | Positioning the storyboard table as a core tool for non-fiction information rhythm |

## Usage

This skill pack serves as a non-fiction technique reference for the ToonFlow Director AI, automatically loaded during the story creation workflow to guide:

1. **Narrative Planning** — Determine thematic core, information density curve, evidence presentation structure, and sound/music strategy
2. **Storyboard Generation** — Output specific shot sizes, camera movements, timing, and transition plans based on narrative planning

The two files have a clear division of labor and build on each other, forming a complete closed loop from "why present it this way" to "how exactly to present it."
