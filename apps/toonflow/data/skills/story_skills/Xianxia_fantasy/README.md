# Ancient Xianxia Fantasy · Director's Narrative Techniques Skill Pack

## Introduction

This skill pack provides a complete reference of director's narrative techniques for the **Ancient Xianxia Fantasy** genre, covering full-process guidance from macro narrative planning to micro storyboard execution. Applicable to any visual style.

## Core Principles

- **Artistic Conception First** — The soul of Xianxia narrative is "meaning beyond the image"; every mountain, river, and cloud is an emotional expression
- **Heaven, Earth, and Humanity as One** — Character emotions merge with celestial phenomena and nature: sorrow brings wind, anger brings thunder, enlightenment brings a burst of heavenly light
- **Fate and Choice** — Narrative tension comes from the pull between "destiny" and "defying heaven"; the more inescapable the fate, the more moving the struggle
- **Blank Space as the Great Way** — The deepest truth and the strongest emotion are not spoken — they lie in a jug of wine, a sword, and silence in the snow

## Usage

This skill pack serves as a narrative technique reference for the ToonFlow director AI, automatically loaded during the story creation workflow to guide:

1. **Narrative Planning** — Determine Xianxia core, fate rhythm curve, scene artistic conception, and music direction
2. **Storyboard Generation** — Output specific shot scales, camera movements, durations, and transition plans based on narrative planning

The two files have clear division of labor and progressive layers, forming a complete closed loop from "why shoot this way" to "how to shoot it specifically."
