# Sweet Romance · Director's Narrative Techniques Skill Pack

## Introduction

This skill pack provides a complete reference of director's narrative techniques for the **Sweet Romance** genre, covering full-process guidance from macro narrative planning to micro storyboard execution. Applicable to any visual style.

## Core Principles

- **Subtlety and Restraint** — Emotional expression relies on blank spaces and subtle reactions, not dialogue exposition
- **Small Details, Big Impact** — Move people with details: a glance, a hesitation to speak, a collar blown askew by the wind
- **Push-Pull Rhythm** — Approach → retreat → approach again → misunderstanding → separation → reunion, each round deeper and sweeter
- **The Power of Silence** — At key emotional moments, silence is more powerful than music

## Usage

This skill pack serves as a narrative technique reference for the ToonFlow director AI, automatically loaded during the story creation workflow to guide:

1. **Narrative Planning** — Determine emotional core, rhythm curve, scene mood, and music direction
2. **Storyboard Generation** — Output specific shot scales, camera movements, durations, and transition plans based on narrative planning

The two files have clear division of labor and progressive layers, forming a complete closed loop from "why shoot this way" to "how to shoot it specifically."
