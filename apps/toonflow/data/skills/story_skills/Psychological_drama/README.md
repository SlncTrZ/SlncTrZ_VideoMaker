# Psychological Drama · Director's Narrative Technique Skill Pack

## Introduction

This skill pack provides a complete set of director's narrative technique references for **psychological drama** genre stories, covering full-process guidance from macro narrative planning to micro storyboard execution. Suitable for any visual style.

## Core Concepts

- **Calm Surface, Turbulent Undercurrent** — The core appeal of psychological drama lies in "what is said and what is thought are different." Every sentence has subtext, every smile could be a trap
- **Information is a Weapon** — Whoever controls information controls the initiative. Narrative tension comes from information asymmetry, concealment, and revelation
- **Multi-Layer Gameplay** — The highest level of psychological drama is not A tricking B, but "A knows B is lying, B knows A knows, A knows B knows A knows" — a cognitive nesting doll
- **The Grayness of Human Nature** — In psychological drama narratives, there are no absolute good or bad people. Every character has their own logic and circumstances

## Usage

This skill pack serves as a narrative technique reference for the ToonFlow Director AI, automatically loaded during the story creation process to guide:

1. **Narrative Planning** — Determine the core theme, information manipulation strategy, emotional rhythm curve, and scene atmosphere direction
2. **Storyboard Generation** — Output specific shot scale, camera movement, duration, and transition plans based on narrative planning

The two files have clear division of labor and progressive layering, forming a complete closed loop from "why shoot this way" to "how to shoot it specifically."
