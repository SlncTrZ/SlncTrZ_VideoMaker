# Urban Workplace Drama · Director's Narrative Techniques Skill Pack

## Introduction

This skill pack provides a complete reference of director's narrative techniques for the **Urban Workplace Drama** genre, covering full-process guidance from macro narrative planning to micro storyboard execution. Applicable to any visual style.

## Core Principles

- **Authenticity is Power** — The killer feature of workplace narrative is "a sense of reality," making the audience see themselves in the characters
- **Undercurrent of Gameplay** — Politeness on the surface, battles underneath; workplace conflict is not in fists but in the struggle for voice and interests
- **The Cost of Growth** — Every upgrade comes with a price (compromise / loss / self-doubt). The growth arc is not a straight line upward but a spiral
- **The Light of Ordinary People** — What moves most is not the power plays of the big boss, but the faint light of ordinary people maintaining their integrity within the system

## Usage

This skill pack serves as a narrative technique reference for the ToonFlow director AI, automatically loaded during the story creation workflow to guide:

1. **Narrative Planning** — Determine workplace core, gameplay rhythm curve, scene power relations, and sound direction
2. **Storyboard Generation** — Output specific shot scales, camera movements, durations, and transition plans based on narrative planning

The two files have clear division of labor and progressive layers, forming a complete closed loop from "why shoot this way" to "how to shoot it specifically."
