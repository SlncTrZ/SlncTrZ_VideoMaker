# Hot-Blooded Youth · Director's Narrative Technique Skill Pack

## Introduction

This skill pack provides a complete set of director's narrative technique references for **hot-blooded youth** genre stories, covering full-process guidance from macro narrative planning to micro storyboard execution. Suitable for any visual style.

## Core Concepts

- **Climax Progression** — Being hot-blooded isn't about constant high intensity, but climbing step by step from a low point to an eruption. The greater the contrast, the stronger the burning sensation
- **Belief Visualization** — Abstract beliefs and willpower must be externalized through concrete actions, gazes, and scars, allowing the audience to "see" the determination
- **Weak Overcoming Strong** — The core charm comes from "doing what one knows is impossible yet still doing it." The more desperate the predicament, the more thrilling the comeback
- **Comrades in Resonance** — Being hot-blooded isn't fighting alone. The trust, sacrifice, and return of comrades are the strongest tear-jerking climaxes

## Usage

This skill pack serves as a narrative technique reference for the ToonFlow Director AI, automatically loaded during the story creation process to guide:

1. **Narrative Planning** — Determine the hot-blooded core, climax rhythm curve, scene emotions, and music eruption timing
2. **Storyboard Generation** — Output specific shot scale, camera movement, duration, and transition plans based on narrative planning

The two files have clear division of labor and progressive layering, forming a complete closed loop from "why shoot this way" to "how to shoot it specifically."
