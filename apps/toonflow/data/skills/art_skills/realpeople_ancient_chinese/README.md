# Real-Person Ancient Chinese Realistic Style Description

This style is exclusively designed for the "real-person ancient Chinese realistic" theme. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Ancient Chinese world view**: Set against ancient China as background, covering residences, palaces, artifacts, costumes, weapons, etc.,eliminate modern elements.
- **Real-person realistic**: All characters, scenes, and props are based on real-person realistic photography standards, emphasizingextreme/ultimate details andreal texture of facial features, skin, hair strands, materials, etc.
- **Ancient Chinese realistic documentary**: Pursues a cinematic atmosphere on top of realism, with strong light-dark contrast, rich details, and an overall atmosphere of cool mixed with warmth, sparse yet dense.
- **Sweet romance atmosphere**: Suitable for conveying gentle, healing, and romantic ancient Chinese love stories, emphasizing emotional expression under Eastern aesthetics.

## Scope of Application

- AI art generation for real-person ancient Chinese themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creations requiringextreme/ultimate realism,fine/delicate texture, and Eastern classical atmosphere

## Prohibited Content

- Cartoon, anime, illustration,anime style style
- Modern elements, modern clothing, modern scenes
- High-saturation fluorescent colors, neon colors
- Suggestive, revealing,see-through and other inappropriate content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Characters with delicate features, a cool or gentle temperament, with costumes, makeup, and hairstyles in ancient Chinese realistic style
- Rich spatial layering in scenes, ultra-clear material textures, realistic and poetic atmosphere
- Props, weapons, accessories, etc., all in ancient Chinese styling, with fine craftsmanship andextreme/ultimate texture
- Storyboards and videos based on real-person realism, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style applies only to "real-person ancient Chinese realistic" related projects. For other styles (such as illustration, anime, modern, etc.), do not use this set of prompts.
