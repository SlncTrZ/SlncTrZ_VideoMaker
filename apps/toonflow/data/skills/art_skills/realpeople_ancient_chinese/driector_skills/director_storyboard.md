## Light Atmosphere Word Bank (Real-Person Ancient Chinese Realistic)

> **Design Principle**: The frame lighting is divided into two layers: **ambient light (main)** and **equipment light (supplementary)**. Ambient light is determined by scene time, weather, and space, and is the absolute main tone of frame lighting. Equipment light is only used forcharacter shaping and emotional enhancement, must obey the ambient light direction andcolor temperature, and must not create an independent light source feel.

### I. Ambient Light (Main Light Source · Scene Natural Light)

> **Each storyboard must first determine the ambient light**, then decide whether to overlay equipment light.

#### Time-of-Day Ambient Light

| Time Period | Natural Light Source | Tonetendency | Ambient Atmosphere |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| Early Morning | Scattered morning light, sky light diffusion | Cool white tone, light blue tone | Thin mistpermeating, air feel, morning dew texture |
| Afternoon | Sunlight oblique, diffused scattered light | Neutral tone, light warm tone | Dappled light and shadow, distinct layering |
| Evening/Dusk | Oblique afterglow, sky light warm-coolalternating | Cool toneas main, horizonpartial warm light | Long stretched shadows, poetic light feel |
| Night | Moonlight cool blue, candle flame/lantern warmaccents | Cool blue main tone | Deep light and shadow, strong light-dark contrast |
| Cloudy/Rainy | Diffused cool light, cloud cover blocking main light source | Gray cool tone | Humid air feel, low saturation |

#### Scene Space Ambient Light

| Space Type | Typical Ambient Light | Light Characteristics |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Outdoor Open | Sky lightas main, sunlight direction determines light and dark | Distant atmospheric perspectivenaturalforming layering |
| Courtyard/Garden | Sky light filtered through branches and leaves,mottled scattering | Under-eave shadows and courtyard light areas form light-dark contrast |
| Indoor (Window Light) | Window one-way natural light, interior gradually darkens | Light side shot,naturalforming light-dark transition |
| Secret/Dark Room | Candle flame/oil lamppartial warm light, overall dark tone | Single light source, limited light area, deep shadows |
| Bamboo Grove/Mountain Field | Light through gaps, scattered light spots | High areastranslucent, ground light and shadowinterwoven |
| Waterfront/Lakeside | Sky light + water surface reflected light | Sparkling waves, reflections fill light, hazy feel |

### II. Equipment Light (Supplementary Light · Character Shaping)

> ⚠️ Equipment light **must obey the ambient light tone**: direction consistent with ambient light source,color temperature must not conflict with ambient light, intensity must not exceed ambient light.

#### Equipment Light Types

| Light Type | Function | Applicable Scene | Usage Constraints |
|----------|------|---------|----------|
| Rim Light (Edge Light) |trace character edge, enhancecharacter separation | Backlit/side-backlit environment | Intensity lower than ambient light,color temperature consistent with ambient light |
| Catchlight | Add eye sparkle and life feel | Close-up/near shot withcharacter | Extremely weak, only as finishing touch, must not form obvious light spot |
| Fill Light (Reflected Light) | Brighten dark area details, avoid dead black on face | When strong side light/backlight causes heavy dark areas | Simulate ambient reflection, must not form independent light source feel |
| Top Light Oppression | Create facial shadows, enhance oppressive feel | Interrogation, confrontation, majestic scenes | Must have scene light sourcereasonable quality (lantern, skylight, palace top light) |

#### Emotion → Ambient Light + Equipment Light Coordination

| Emotional Tone | Ambient Light (Main) | Equipment Light (Supplementary) | Coordination Principle |
|----------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Heart-throb / Warmth | Soft side-back natural light, scattered warm tone ambient | Rim lightslight outline | Ambient light sets side-back tone, equipment light only supplements rim, shallow depth of field softens background |
| Confrontation / Oppression | Hard natural side light, high contrast light-dark ambient | Top light can be added (must have reasonable source) | Ambient light sets main direction and hardness, equipment light only enhances contrast, shadows hard |
| Suppression / Sadness | Diffused cool light, cloudy/dark room ambient | Very little fill light,retain facial dark areas | Mainly ambient cool light, deliberately not fill lighting toretain shadow layering |
| Mystery / Solemnity | Cool blue ambient light, backlit silhouette | Rim light precisely controlled, glow restrained | Ambient light sets cool blue tone, equipment light only for edge separation |
| Ethereal / Artistic Conception | Scattered soft light, natural light slightly overexposed | Generally no equipment light needed | Ambient scattered lightas main, distant view light and faint, atmospheric perspective |

### III. Light Conflict Avoidance Rules

> When generating storyboard prompts, strictly follow the following principles to ensurenaturalcan believable frame lighting:

1. **Determine ambient light first, then overlay equipment light**: Each storyboard first determines the ambient light tone based on "time period + scene space", then decides whether equipment light is needed forcharacter shaping.
2. **Color temperature consistency**: Equipment lightcolor temperature must coordinate with ambient light (e.g., cool blue environment at night cannot overlay warm yellow fill light, unless there is a reasonable warm light source within the scene such as candle flame).
3. **Directionreasonable quality **: Equipment light direction must be supported by light sources within the scene (window light direction, candle position, moonlight angle), must not appear out of nowhere.
4. **Intensity subordination**: Equipment light intensity **must not exceed ambient light**,avoid "characterindividual lit" studio feel.
5. **Less is more**: When ambient light is sufficient forcharacter shaping and emotional expression, **do not add equipment light**.

## Aesthetic Prohibitions (Strictly Avoid During Generation)

The following words/styles must not appear in output prompts:

- ❌ Japanese/Koreanfresh filter words (such as: fresh, light fresh, Japanese style)
- ❌ Anime/2D/illustration/CG rendering related words
- ❌ Modern clothing/modern scene elements
- ❌ Warm yellow main tone words (use "partial warm candlelightaccents")
- ❌ Soft focus/hazy feel/low contrast filter words
- ❌ Clashing colors/mixed/neon/fluorescent colors
- ❌ Cartoon proportions, big eyes, chibi and otherdeformation descriptions
- ❌ Cyberpunk/steampunk/Western fantasy elements
- ❌ Overlaid text on frame (subtitles, watermarks, title cards, narrationoverlay text, title text and other UI layer text; the frame must be a pure visual image)

> 💡 **Exception**: Prop text within the story world (character writing, scroll handwriting, plaque, signboard, letter, prescription and othertext naturally present in the scene) **does not fall within the prohibited range**. When such content is included in storyboard scene descriptions, it should betruthfully described andrequire textclear.
