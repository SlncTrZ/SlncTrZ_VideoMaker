# 90s Japanese Anime Style Description

This style is specifically crafted for the "90s Japanese Anime Aesthetic" theme. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **90s Anime Worldview**: Set in a 90s Japanese lifestyle backdrop, covering schools, stations, streets, cafes, residences, etc., embodying the atmosphere of Japanese daily life.
- **Hand-drawn Flat Coloring Aesthetic**: All characters, scenes, and props follow 90s hand-drawn animation standards, emphasizing clear lines, even coloring, and layered shading.
- **Nostalgic Healing Aesthetics**: Pursues cinematic atmosphere on a flat coloring foundation, with soft warm tones, rich details, and an overall warm, healing, nostalgic feel.
- **Emotional Expression**: Suitable for portraying gentle, healing, romantic Japanese love stories, emphasizing emotional expression under Eastern aesthetics.

## Scope

- AI art generation for 90s Japanese anime themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiring ultimate hand-drawn texture, delicate lines, and a warm healing atmosphere

## Prohibited Content

- Modern anime style, 3D rendering, CG animation
- Modern elements, modern architecture, modern clothing
- High-saturation fluorescent colors, neon colors
- Excessive shadows, dark violence, adult content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Characters with delicate facial features, gentle or bright temperament, clothing, makeup, and hairstyles all in 90s Japanese anime style
- Rich spatial layering in scenes, clear flowing lines, authentic and poetic atmosphere
- Props, everyday items, and environmental details all in Japanese design, finely crafted with delicate texture
- Storyboards and visuals based on hand-drawn flat coloring, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style is only applicable to projects related to "90s Japanese Anime." For other styles (such as modern animation, CG, illustration, contemporary, etc.), do not use this set of prompts.
