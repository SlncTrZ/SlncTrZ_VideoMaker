| Dimension | Definition |

## II. Camera Grammar (Replaces "Render Engine")

> Live-Action Urban does not rely on a rendering pipeline. Image quality depends on camera choice, lens character, and light orchestration. Below are the core parameters of "Camera Grammar."

### 2.1 Frame & Format

| Parameter | Baseline Value | Aesthetic Intent |
|---|---|---|
| Aspect Ratio | 1.85:1 (widescreen) or 16:9 | Urban horizontal spatial sense, coexistence of character and environment; vertical (9:16) only for phone narrative contexts |
| Imaging Format | 35mm film texture / Full-frame digital cinematography | Shallow depth of field capability, natural vignette transition, subtle grain structure |
| Color Science | ARRI Alexa / Sony Venice-class cinematic color science | Natural skin tone reproduction, smooth highlight roll-off, detailed shadows without crushing |

### 2.2 Lens Character

| Focal Length | Narrative Character | Typical Application |
|---|---|---|
| 24-28mm (Wide) | Environment enveloping character, spatial alienation, urban grandeur vs. individual insignificance | Urban empty shots, loneliness in crowds, relationship between architecture and people |
| 35mm (Slight Wide) | Subjective viewpoint, immersive realism | Street following, indoor dialogue, daily walking |
| 50mm (Standard) | Equivalent to human eye, objective observation, neitherbeautify nor distorting | Character portraits, two-person dialogue, emotional gaze |
| 85mm (Medium Tele) | Compressing space, closing emotional distance, background blurhighlight character | Close-ups, voyeuristic perspectives, seeing from across the street |
| 135mm+ (Telephoto) | Extreme compression, voyeuristic feel, isolating character from environment | Isolated individuals in the city, distant gazing, physical distance of emotion |

### 2.3 Camera Position & Movement

| Technique | Visual Psychology | Usage Scenario |
|---|---|---|
| Fixed Position | Calm, observational, letting time speak for itself | Daily scenes, dialogue, empty shot pauses |
| Handheld Slight Shake | Presence, breathing quality, subjectiveintervention | Emotional fluctuation, street walking, intimate following |
| Steadicam/Gimbal | Elegant flow, poetry of urban wandering | Important scene transitions, character entrance, spacedisplay |
| High Angle | Fatefulness, insignificance, god's perspective | Cityoverlook, endinglook back, characterpredicament |
| Low Angle | Oppression or sublimity | Skyscrapersense of oppression, character's sense of power |
| Rack Focus | Shift of attention, change in relationship | Focus wandering during character eye contact, revealing hidden information |

## III. City Spectrum — Real Colors of Light and Materials

> The colors of Live-Action Urban come from the real light and materials of Chinese cities, not from an engine palette. The following palette is extracted from: reflections on office building glass curtain walls, the years on old neighborhood cement walls, rainy nights under streetlights, convenience store fluorescents.

### 3.1 Urban Light Source Spectrum

| Light Source | Color Temperature (K) | Light Color Description | Urban Domain |
|---|---|---|---|
| Morning Light (1h after sunrise) | 3500-4500 | Low-angle warm gold diffusion, thin fog qualitydiffusion light | Empty early subway cars, street cleaner's roads, breakfast stall steam |
| Morning Skylight | 5500-6000 | Cool clear, sharp shadows, strongest building surface texture | Office building glass reflections, clean street reflections, commutercrowd flow |
| Noon Top Light | 6000-6500 | Hard cold strong light, short dense shadows, deep shadows under eyes/jaw | Office building atriums, exposed streets — use with caution |
| Golden Hour (1h before sunset) | 2800-3500 | Most precious warm tone, long shadows, every surface gilded | First choice for all warm and memorysegment |
| Blue Hour (20min after sunset) | 8000-10000 | Sky deep blue-purple, city lights still weaker than skylight, cool poetic | Solitude, transition, inner monologue, closing |
| High-Pressure Sodium Lamp (Old streetlights) | 2000-2200 | Signature warm orange-yellow, the warmth of the city night | Old town alleys, late night solitary walks, nostalgic texture |
| LED Streetlight | 4000-5000 | Cool white or slightly blue, modern city's calm face | New districts, under overpasses, night scenes around office buildings |
| Convenience Store/Subway Fluorescent | 4000-6500 | Cold but safe white light, island of late-night city | Late-night convenience stores, last subway, duty rooms |
| Screen Light (Phone/Computer/Ad screen) | Varies | Cool blue or warm white face light, illuminating one side of face, companion of solitude | Solitary moments, insomnia, alienation of the information age |
| Car Headlight Beam | 3000 (halogen warm) / 6000 (LED cool) | Moving point light source, volumetric effect when beam passes through rain/mist | Street night scenes, car chasing, passing by |

### 3.2 Urban Material Spectrum

| Material | Color Range | Location in the City |
|---|---|---|
| Glass Curtain Wall | Cyan-gray → Deep blue → Gold/orange reflection of sky | CBD skyline, office buildings, shopping malls |
| Concrete/Cement | Beige-gray → Cool gray → Dark gray after rain, with water stains and moss marks | Old neighborhood facades, bridges, underpasses |
| Red Brick | Ochre red → Dark brick red, weathered to gray-brown | Old town residential buildings, campus old buildings, creative parks |
| Asphalt Road Surface | Dark gray near black, mirror-like reflection after rain | All city roads |
| Stainless Steel/Aluminum Alloy | Cool silver-gray, brushed or mirror reflection | Office building lobbies, subway cars, bus stops |
| Street Tree Green | Spring fresh green → Summer deep green → Autumn caramel/golden | Streets, parks, residential areas |
| White Wall (Indoor) | Warm whiteleaning yellowish (old buildings) / Cool whiteleaning blue (new renovations) | Rental apartments, offices, hospitals |

### 3.3 Narrative Emotional Palette

> Instead of mechanically assigning "main color/accent color," define the visual temperature of a scene by the "emotion of light."

| Emotional Scene | Light Recipe | Color Temperature Direction | Visual Keywords |
|---|---|---|---|
| Morning Departure | Morning light 45° angle + thin fog diffusion | Cool base warm light (3600K daylight + 5500K ambient skylight) | Clear, hope, quiet, gathering momentum |
| Office Daily | Window light primary + screen cool light filling face | Neutral to slightly cool (5000K baseline) | Order, focus, restraint, detachment |
| Afternoon Relaxation | Window light wide diffusion + curtain softening | Warm white (4500K) | Lazy, healing, everyday poetry |
| Intimate Approach | Warm light side light (desk lamp/candle/evening glow) + shadowretain details | Very warm (2800-3200K) | Warmth, dissolving distance, unspoken words |
| Street Life | Mixed light sources (warm streetlight + cool store white + car lights sweeping) | Warm dominant (3000K warm +partial cool contrast) | Lively, real, human warmth, warmth in crowds |
| Rainy Night Solitary Walk | Wet ground reflecting streetlights + rain curtain scattering + distant neon blurred | Cool base (6000K) + warm patches (2800K) accents | Loneliness, contemplation, relationship between city and individual |
| Late Night Vulnerability | Single light source (desk lamp/phone screen/window streetlight) island lighting | Spot warm (3000K) sinking into darkness (no-light areas pure black) | Fragility, authenticity, dropping the mask, one's own moment |
| Conflict Confrontation | Hard light side lighting + deep shadows + cool-warm contrast (window cool/indoor warm) | Split (cool/warm dual color temperature not blending) | Tension, confrontation, relationshiprupture |
| Farewell Moment | Backlight (character silhouetted) + long shadows + blue hour sky | Cool (sky 8000K) + warm (streetlight 2800K) farewell-style cool-warm coexistence | Reluctance, release, end of a chapter |

## IV. Global Constraint Rules

### 4.1 Image Authenticity Constraints (Mandatory)

| No. | Rule |
|---|---|
| P1 | Must anchor on "live-action photography / live-action footage / live-action film still" — the image must read as "shot by a camera," not "generated by a computer" |
| P2 | Must specify a concrete light source logic: where does the light for this scene come from? (window/lamp/sky/screen) Light source direction, color temperature, and hardness must be self-consistent |
| P3 | Must retain thereal texture of character skin — pores, fine lines, moles, natural skin tone unevenness —eliminate plastic smoothing and AI mask face |
| P4 | Hair must appear natural — blown by wind, wet by rain, loose strands when tied up —eliminate modeling-styleneat hair blocks |
| P5 | Clothing must show signs of being "worn" — fabric wrinkles, slight pilling, naturally deformed collar, worn pant hems —eliminate brand new sample clothes feel |
| P6 | Scene must have evidence of being "used" — items naturally scattered on desktop, traces of life on walls, imperfect floors —eliminate showroom |
| P7 | Character must be in natural posture — real standing stance (weight on one side), unposed expression (not perfectly symmetrical smile), natural hand movements |
| P8 | Must specify a concrete contemporary Chinese urban space — refuse "generic city" or any empty empty city without national identifiers |

### 4.2 Photography Quality Constraints (Mandatory)

| No. | Rule |
|---|---|
| Q1 | Must declare a camera "presence mode": fixed gaze / handheld breathing / gimbal flow / telephoto voyeurism |
| Q2 | Depth of field must have narrative intent — shallow DoF tohighlight emotion, deep DoF to emphasize environment, rack focus to guide attention |
| Q3 | Highlights must not be blown out to white, shadows must not be crushed to black — preserving highlight roll-off and shadow detail is the foundation of cinematic feel |
| Q4 | Must declare grain preference: 35mm film grain (fine irregular) / digital clean sharp (default) |
| Q5 | Color tonality must match the emotional scene — warm withoutcolor overflow, cool without lookingdrab, neon without looking cheap |

### 4.4 Style Boundary Judgment

> When the generated image shows any of the following signals, it has deviated from the Live-Action Urban style:

| Boundary Signal | Explanation |
|---|---|
| Character skin feels like silicone | Over-smoothed,lose live-action texture — immediately stop using "smooth," "perfect skin" type vocabulary |
| Light source origin unclear | Image is bright but can't tell where the light comes from — supplement light source logic, reject "uniform global brightness" |
| Buildings without Chinese signage | Cityhas lost Chinese identity — add shop signs, street signs, bus stops and otherclear Chinese urban symbols |
| Image too clean | Scene looks like just renovated — add traces of life |
| Character stance like a model | Posturestiff symmetrical — give a natural center of gravityoffset |
| Colors look like a filter APP | Tone uniform but cheap — return to light source color temperaturedrive, stop "filtermechanically apply" |
