# Live-Action Urban Prop Image Generation · Constraint Manual

---

## I. Prop Design Principles

> Live-action urban props are not "modeled objects" but "used items" — it was in someone's hand, placed on some desk, carried in some pocket. The camera captures its present state.

1. **Object is Narrative** — Every prop is a miniature narrative container. Lipstick mark on coffee cup implies someone just drank from it, phone case wear implies long use, umbrella fold marks imply repeated opening and closing
2. **Use Traces Over Perfect Condition** — Live-action urban props must have evidence of "being used." Brand new, just unboxed, showroom-grade props never appear
3. **Real Contemporary Chinese Urban Items** — Props must be real objects existing in Chinese urban life: Alipay/WeChat Pay interfaces, domestic phone brands, Chinese packaging, Chinese-style takeout containers — reject country-less fictional items
4. **Material is Real** — Not PBR parameters rendered materials, but real material behavior captured by camera: stainless steel reflection, ceramic glaze, paper's light absorption, plastic's injection texture
5. **Single-View Still-Life Photography** — Prop presented independently as a single still-life product photo, not multi-angle modelingdisplaying, not 2×2 four-grid

## III. Single-View Still-Life Photography Specifications

> Live-action urban props only need a single still-life product photo, not multi-angle modelingdisplaying. Below is the complete specification for single-view photography.

### Frame Composition

| Item | Photography Requirement |
|---|---|
| Prop presentation | Prop centered or on rule of thirds position, independently displayed, complete in frame, no cropping |
| Background | Plain gray seamless backdrop paper #B0B0B0 (matte), or pure white desk/tabletop (light wood grain/cream white desk), chosen per prop attributes. No extra objects, no decoration, no text |
| Lighting | Natural window light or single soft light. Window light 45° side entry (bright soft with natural shadow), or softbox top+side fill (product photography grade even light). Soft even light, clear direction, no hard shadows |
| Depth of field | Shallow depth of field (f/2.8-f/5.6), prop subjectclear sharp, foreground and background subtly blurredemphasize subject |
| Shadow | Natural contact shadow between prop bottom and surface — real not cutout or floating. Shadow soft, direction matching main light source |
| Aspect ratio | Recommend 1:1 square or 4:3, suitable single propdisplaying |

### Prop State Guidelines

| Dimension | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Usetraces | Must have visual evidence of "being used" — not brand new sealed, not sample condition |
| Chinese recognizability | Chinese signage/Chinese brand/Chinese-style packaging/contemporary Chinese daily — reject country-less fictional items |
| Material realism | Not rendered texture — is the real material behavior captured by camera (reflection, diffusion, transmission of light) |
| Scale feel | Through material texture density, craft detailimplying real size, no reference object needed |

## IV. Materials and Surfaces — Real Objects Under the Camera

> Below are descriptions of how camera captures the surface behavior of urban daily items, not render parameters.

| Material | Real Performance Under Camera | Prompt |
|---|---|---|
| Metal (stainless steel/aluminum alloy) | Brushed texture visible, edge highlight sharp but not dead white, reflecting surrounding soft environment color, surface with fingerprints and micro-scratches | Metal brushed texture, natural environment reflection, surface micro-scratches and fingerprinttraces, non-rendered luster |
| Glass | Translucent but with faint own color (glass cross-section pale green), surface with fingerprints or water stains, edge chamfer with light refraction | Glass natural translucent, surface real finger/water stains, edge light refraction, non-CG perfect transparent |
| Ceramic | Glaze soft reflection (not mirror), surface with fine crackle/scratches, cup rim and bottom with use wear | Ceramic glaze soft reflection, fine use scratches, cup bottom natural wear |
| Plastic | Injection texture/parting line visible, surface with fine scratches, matte plastic's diffuse reflection texture | Injection textureclear, parting line natural visible, surface fine use scratches |
| Paper | Fiber roughness visible, fold natural (not deliberate), printed text subtly bleeding, edges with reading wear | Paper fiber texture, natural folds, printed text real texture, edge natural wear |
| Fabric (cotton/linen/canvas) | Weave texture visible, surface with fuzz/pilling, folds and use wrinkles, slight color unevenness | Fabric weave texture, natural fuzz and pilling, real post-use wrinkles |
| Leather | Natural grain/pores visible, bend area with usetraces, edge with natural wear, hardware with oxidation | Leather natural grain, bend usetraces, hardware slight oxidation, real use patina |
| Wood | Natural wood grain, surface with scratches/dents, paint with use wear, color subtly changed | Wood grain natural clear, surface use denttraces, paint daily wear |

## VI. Constraint Rules

### Mandatory

| # | Rule |
|---|---|
| R1 | Must be **single** still-life photography — single view, not multi-angle, not 2×2 four-grid, not modelingdisplaying |
| R2 | Must specify "plain gray backdrop paper #B0B0B0" or "light wood grain desk/cream white tabletop" and other real surfaces, prohibit complex scenes |
| R3 | Must specify light source logic — window light or softbox, clear light direction, natural shadow |
| R4 | Prop must have "usetraces" — not brand new, not sample condition, not unsealed |
| R5 | Prop must have contemporary Chinese urban recognizability — Chinese signage/Chinese brand/Chinese-style packaging |
| R6 | Must declare "product photography + non-3D render non-CG" core anchor |
| R7 | Material must be described as real performance under camera, not render parameters |

### Strictly Prohibited

| # | Strictly Prohibited |
|---|---|
| X1 | Strictly prohibit "3D render / 3D modeling / CG / UE engine / Blender / PBR material" and all CG terminology |
| X2 | Strictly prohibit "2D hand-drawn / illustration / animation / anime" and all non-photographic media |
| X3 | Strictly prohibit "multi-angle / four views / 2×2 grid / orthographic view / turnaround / blueprint" — single only |
| X4 | Strictly prohibit props that are "brand new sealed / sample condition / no usetraces / perfect condition" |
| X5 | Strictly prohibit "ancient style/period/xianxia/wuxia/Republican era/cyberpunk/sci-fi/Western fantasy/ fictional " and all non-contemporary urban items |
