# 2D Flat Design Style Description

This style is specifically crafted for the "2D Flat Design" theme. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Flat Design Style**: Centered on Flat Design, covering UI design, infographics, illustrations, etc., embodying modern minimalist aesthetics.
- **Pure Color Block Texture**: All characters, scenes, and props follow 2D flat style standards, emphasizing pure color fills, no shadows or gradients, and clean lines.
- **Minimalist Modern Aesthetics**: Pursues clear expression on a flat design foundation, with bright clean tones, simplified details, and an overall atmosphere that is minimalist, modern, and comfortable.
- **Emotional Expression**: Suitable for portraying gentle, healing, romantic flat-style content, emphasizing emotional expression under modern minimalist aesthetics.

## Scope

- AI art generation for 2D flat design themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiring ultimate flat texture, clean lines, and modern minimalist atmosphere

## Prohibited Content

- Realistic rendering, 3D rendering, photorealistic quality
- Shadows, gradients, textures, lighting effects
- Complex details, fine textures, realistic backgrounds
- Dark violence, adult content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Character faces are simple with a fresh temperament; shapes and costumes are in 2D flat design style
- Scene space layers are clean, lines are clear and flowing, atmosphere is authentic and modern
- Props, everyday items, and environmental details are all flat-style shapes, simply crafted with refreshing texture
- Storyboards and visuals are based on 2D flat design, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style is only applicable to projects related to "2D Flat Design." For other styles (such as realistic 3D, illustration, modern 3D, etc.), do not use this set of prompts.
