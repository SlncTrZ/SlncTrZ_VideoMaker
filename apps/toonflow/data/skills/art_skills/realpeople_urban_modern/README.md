# Live-Action Urban Photorealistic Style Description

This style is specially crafted for the "Live-Action Urban Photorealistic" genre. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Modern Worldview**: Set in modern urban environments, covering offices, cafés, homes, streets, shopping malls, etc.,eliminate missing modern elements.
- **Live-Action Photorealism**: All characters, scenes, and props adhere to live-action photographic standards, emphasizingextreme/ultimate detail andreal texture in facial features, skin, hair, and materials.
- **Urban Documentary Realism**: Pursues cinematic-grade atmosphere on a realistic foundation, with strong light-dark contrast, rich detail, and an overall warm, straightforward, modern-life feel.
- **Sweet Romance Atmosphere**: Suitable for showcasing gentle, healing, romantic urban love stories, emphasizing emotional expression within a modern urban context.

## Scope of Application

- AI art generation for live-action urban themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiringextreme/ultimate photorealism,fine/delicate texture, and modern urban atmosphere

## Prohibited Content

- Cartoon, anime, illustration, anime style
- Missing modern elements, ancient style/ancient costume/traditional architecture
- High saturation fluorescent colors, neon colors
- Suggestive, revealing, see-through, or other inappropriate content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Refined character facial features, gentle or capable temperament, all attire, makeup, and hairstyles in modern urban realistic style
- Rich spatial layering in scenes,ultra-clear material textures, authentic atmosphere full of life
- Props, phones, computers, office supplies, etc., all with modern styling, fine craftsmanship, andextreme/ultimate texture
- Storyboards and videos based on live-action photorealism, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style is only applicable to "Live-Action Urban Photorealistic" related projects. For other styles (such as ancient, illustration, anime, modern, etc.), do not use this set of prompts.
