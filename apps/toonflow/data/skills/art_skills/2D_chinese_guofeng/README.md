# Chinese-style Anime Neo-Chic Style Description

This style is specifically crafted for the "Chinese-style Anime Neo-Chic" theme. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Chinese-style Anime Animation**: Anime style set against the backdrop of traditional Chinese culture, covering genres such as xianxia (fantasy), wuxia (martial arts), and courtly themes
- **Neo-Chic Aesthetics**: Combining traditional Eastern elements with modern aesthetics, balancing classical charm with contemporary sensibility
- **Japanese Animation Rendering Techniques**: Modern Japanese anime coloring and lighting techniques, cel shading combined with digital rendering
- **Eastern Classical Charm**: Poetic atmosphere, negative space composition, profound Eastern aesthetic expression
- **Delicate Brushwork**: Clear lines, fine details, exquisite character and scene design
- **Chinese Anime Cinematic Quality**: Cinematic composition, lighting layers, dynamic tension, emotional atmosphere

## Scope

- AI art generation for Chinese-style anime themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiring neo-chic aesthetics, Japanese anime rendering, and Chinese-style artistic conception
- Chinese-style animation, game art, concept design, illustration creation

## Prohibited Content

- Fully photorealistic photography / 3D realistic rendering
- Western fantasy / Gothic / Victorian style
- Cyberpunk / sci-fi / overly modern elements
- Rough lines / blurry quality / low-precision modeling
- Chaotic colors / inconsistent style / aesthetic breakdown

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Character designs in anime proportions with exquisite traditional Chinese clothing, delicate flowing lines
- Scenes filled with Eastern artistic conception, rich traditional architectural details, exquisite light and shadow rendering
- Props and artifacts meticulously researched, clear material textures, harmonious and unified colors
- Storyboards and rendering emphasizing cinematic quality, neo-chic aesthetics, and Japanese animation techniques
