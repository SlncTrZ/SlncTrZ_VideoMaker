# Stop-Motion Clay Style Description

This style is exclusively designed for the "stop-motion clay texture" theme. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Stop-motion world view**: Set against a retro/fantasy life backdrop, featuring log cabins, forests, streets, cafes, gardens, etc., embodying the unique living atmosphere of stop-motion animation.
- **Clay texture**: All characters, scenes, and props are based on stop-motion clay animation standards, emphasizing clear clay texture, visible finger impressions, and material graininess.
- **Healing nostalgic aesthetic**: Pursues a cinematic atmosphere on top of the clay texture, with warm and soft color tones, rich details, and an overall warm, healing, and nostalgic feel.
- **Emotional expression**: Suitable for conveying gentle, healing, and romantic stop-motion stories, emphasizing emotional expression through handcrafted textures.

## Scope of Application

- AI art generation for stop-motion clay themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creations requiring extreme clay texture, delicate lighting, and a warm healing atmosphere

## Prohibited Content

- Modern 3D animation style, smooth plastic, CG rendering
- Modern elements, modern architecture, modern clothing
- High-saturation fluorescent colors, neon colors
- Dark, violent, adult-oriented content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Characters with delicate features, gentle or bright temperament, with costumes and styling in stop-motion clay animation style
- Rich spatial layering in scenes, clear clay texture, realistic and poetic atmosphere
- Props, everyday items, and environmental details in retro/fantasy styling with delicate handcrafted texture
- Storyboards and visuals based on stop-motion animation, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style applies only to "stop-motion clay" related projects. For other styles (such as modern 3D, CG, illustration, modern, etc.), do not use this set of prompts.
