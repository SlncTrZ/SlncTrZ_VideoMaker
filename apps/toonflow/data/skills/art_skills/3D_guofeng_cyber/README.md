## I. Core Style Definition (Generation underlying logic)
All art prompts, specifications, and generated content under this style are strictly limited to:
- **Chinese cyber 3D rendering**: Centered on 3D modeling rendering technology, deeply integrating traditional Eastern aesthetics with cyberpunk sci-fi core, fusing Chinese mortise-and-tenon joints, ink-wash artistic conception, traditional patterns, ancient forms with holographic lighting, mechanical structures, neon light effects, and futuristic tech elements through localized fusion, rejecting element stacking without core
- **3D ultra-fine texture**: 8K high-precision model, 4K ultra-clear PBR physical material rendering, next-gen game-level topology, metal/fabric/jade/holographic material texture maximized, details vivid, textures clearly discernible
- **Chinese cyberexclusive color palette**: Using turquoise, vermillion, indigo, gilt, ink black as base, paired with low-saturation Chinese neon colors (cinnabar red neon, azurite blue holographic, gilt glow),eliminate fluorescent color bleeding, elegant colors with futuristic feel
- **Eastern cyber aesthetic atmosphere**: Ink-wash artistic conception of virtual and real interwoven + cyber lighting layering, negative space composition, Chinese symmetrical aesthetics, misty volumetric light intertwined with holographic projection, perfect fusion of poetic space and futuristic city feel
- **Chinese cybercharacter styling**: 3D next-gen ancient style modeling, Eastern classical bone structure, costumes in Hanfu form + cyber functionmodification, makeup elegantfusion cyber light effect makeup, hairstyles refinedmatching mechanical hair accessories,throughout preserving Eastern charm
- **Cinematictop-tier lighting**: UE5/Blender Cyclestop-tier rendering, volumetric light, ambient occlusion, global illumination, ray tracing, depth of field blur, motion blur, LUT color calibration, cinematic lens language, atmosphere maximized

### Scope of Application
- AI art generation for Chinese cyber 3D themes (character illustrations, scene concepts, prop design, storyboard scripts, game CG, film posters, etc.)
- Visual creations requiring 3D next-gen texture, Eastern aesthetic core, and cyber sci-fi atmosphere
- Commercial application scenarios such as Chinese cyber themed game art, film concept design, animated shorts, digital collectibles, etc.

### Prohibited Content
- Realistic photography/photorealistic realism (unless specifically noted)
- Pure Western cyberpunk/Western fantasy/Gothic/Victorian style, rigid splicing without Chinese core
- Modern urban everyday elements/pure sci-fi machinery without Chinese elements
- Low-precision models/rough topology/blurry textures/clipping/deformed structures
- Oversaturated fluorescent colors/high color bleeding/strong digital noise/low contrast hazy images
- Western modeling faces violating Eastern classical bone structure/deformed human bodies/discordant exaggerated limb proportions
- Vulgar revealing content/costumesheavy modification not conforming to Hanfu forms/element stacking without cultural core
