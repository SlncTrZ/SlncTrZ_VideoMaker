## I. Color System and Visual Tone
(Dual-scene self-check: Ancient scenes/Urban scenes both haveclear execution standards, no adaptation blind spots)

- **Color base** — The entire film uses moon white (C1), turquoise (C2), indigo (C4) as Eastern traditional base colors,overlay Chinese-style adapted cyber core colors (vermillion neon, turquoise fluorescent, indigo cyber light, gilt holographic color); overallcolor temperature neutralleaning cool (4500-5800K), traditional base saturation medium-high (55-75%), cyber overlay color saturationcan control pulling high (70-90%). **【Ancient scenes】** Use base colors as core colors for architecture, costumes, props; **【Urban scenes】** Use the same color system as base colors forskyscraper facades, cityscapes, public spaces, costumes and props, with cyber overlay colors uniformly anchored to the Eastern traditional color system,eliminate cluttered urban neon without Chinese anchor points.
- **Emotion color palette driven** — Six dual-sceneuniversal emotion color palettescorresponding different narrativesegment, each palette configured with a "traditional base layer + cyber overlay layer" dual-track system, palette switches shouldfully synchronized with the story arc:
 1. Palace magnificence/Urban power center
 2. Landscape artistic conception/Urban sky garden
 3. Boudoir gentleness/Urban private residence
 4. Martial arts killing intent/Urban workplace confrontation/Crime investigationgame/strategy
 5. Festive joy/Urban street festival/Night life narrative
 6. Moonlit quietude/Urban night solitude/City empty shots
- **Warm/cool narrative contrast** — Traditional warm colors (vermillion C3, golden yellow C5, rouge C7, gamboge C9) + cyber warm light (neon vermillion, holographic gilt) as visual signals for narrativeturn/twist, used for emotionalrising warm and festivesegment; traditional cool colors (indigo C4, ink black C6) + cyber cool light (fluorescent turquoise, cyber indigo) used for killing intent, melancholy, quietsegment; cyber light brightness/color switching can serve as visual anchor for strong narrativeturn/twist, **【Ancient scenes】** primarily traditional colors with cyber as secondary, **【Urban scenes】** can balance dual-track colorweight balance based on narrative needs.
- **Palette-first principle** —segment planning must first bind emotional scenarios, thendetermine/confirm "traditional main color + secondary color + cyber overlay color" and lighting scheme,avoid "correct plot but wrong emotional color", ensuringcompletelyunified color logic for both ancient/urban scenes.
- **Prohibited color range** — High-saturationcluttered fluorescent colors without Chinese adaptation, discordant modern digital colors, low-quality cheap neon colors, Western cyberpunknonelogic clashing colors are all incompatible with this style; **【Urban scenes】** additionally prohibited: street advertisementcolor jump without Chinese anchor,cluttered street neon, pure industrial cold gray color system.

## III. Texture Direction
(Dual-scene self-check: Full coverage of ancient/urban materials,clear dual-scene aging texture standards,eliminate plastic feel/CG feel)

- **3D rendering as dual-track anchor** — Core of Chinese cyber 3D: high-precision dual-track modeling, PBR physical material rendering, traditional layer volumetric light + cyber layer holographic light, ambient occlusion, depth of field blur,throughout presenting cinematic 3D rendered images. **【Ancient scenes】** Focus on ancient architecture, traditional costumes and props, natural landscape high-precision modeling; **【Urban scenes】** Focus on new Chineseskyscraper, urban public spaces, Chinese cyber vehicles, urban costumes and props high-precision dual-track modeling, both scene types strictly follow the same PBR rendering standard.
- **PBR material dual-track fusion** — All costumes, props, and scene assets must achievecan believable texture through PBR physical rendering, core is "organic fusion of traditional materials + cyber materials", dual-scene full coverage:
 - **【Ancient core materials】** Silk luster anddraping texture, wood texture and patina, metal reflection and texture, jade translucency and warmth, porcelain glaze luster
 - **【Urban core materials】** Glass curtain wall transparent reflection and Chinese grille light-shadowcut, reinforced concrete texturefusion with traditional stone texture, metal aluminum panel matte texture and traditional copper ornament aged reflection, asphalt road weartraces echoing with bluestone weathering texture, optical fiber fabric flowing light unified with traditional silkdraping texture
 - All fused materials must be physically logically self-consistent,prohibited meaningless cyber material stacking.
- **Volumetric light and depth of field dual-track enhancement** — Volumetric light is the soul of Chinese cyber images: **【Ancient scenes】** Outdoor scenes must have air perspective and dual volumetric light scattering of traditional sky light + cyber neon; indoor scenes createmulti-layer volumetric light effects through window light/candlelight/holographic projection; **【Urban scenes】** Outdoors must have urban canyon street volumetric light,skyscraper cluster sky light diffusion, neon light volumetric scattering; indoors createmulti-layer volumetric light effects through window lattice projection/holographic screens/digitally controlled light sources. Depth of field blursynchronize enhances dual-scene spatial depth, adapting to different spatial scales of ancient/urban scenes.
- **Aging texture dual-track unified** — Materials should not beexcessively clean and perfect, both scenes mustreflect living traces and aging feel:
 - **【Ancient scenes】** Wood surfaces have usagetraces, stone surfaces have weathering patterns and moss, fabric has natural folds, tiles have moss traces andimperfect
 - **【Urban scenes】** Asphalt roads havewheel rut wear, glass curtain walls have water stains and weatheringtraces, metal facades have oxidation and scratches, street facilities have usagetraces, neon signs havepartial flickeringflaw
 -throughoutprohibited "plastic feel", brand new flawless "cheap CG feel", "showroom-style" urban images without living traces.
- **Cyber does not equal cold** — Chinese cyber 3D emphasizes the balance between Eastern aesthetic warmth and cyber sci-fiavant-garde feel, conveying emotions through dual-track material texture, layered lighting, and traditional + cyberfusion color matching, rather than relying on meaningless spectacle effects andviolent cyber element stacking. Both ancient/urban scenes mustadhere to Eastern aesthetic core.
