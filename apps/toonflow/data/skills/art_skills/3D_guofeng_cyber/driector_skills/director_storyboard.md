# Storyboard Prompts · 3D Chinese Cyber · Style-Specific Technique

## Scope of Application
This Skill is dedicated to generating storyboard prompts for the **3D Chinese Cyber (New Chinese Cyberpunk)** style, with the core being "Chinese aesthetics as soul, cyber sci-fi as form" — an organic fusion, **perfectly adapted to two major creative directions**:
1. Traditional ancient scene cyberization (palace, garden, martial arts, sect and other ancient native scenes)
2. Modern urban scene Chinese cyberization (commercial district, office building, apartment, night market and other modern urban scenes)
Reject rigid element splicing,adhere to Chinese aesthetic core.

## Aesthetic Prohibitions (Strictly Avoid During Generation · Dual-scene Adapted Revised Version)

The following words/styles must not appear in output prompts:

- ❌ Realistic photography/photorealistic words (e.g., photorealistic, realistic photography)
- ❌ Messy cluttered high-saturation fluorescent colors/neon without Chinese color logic/digital cheap feel
- ❌ Pure Western cyberpunk/Japanese cyber/pure sci-fi without Chinese core
- ❌ Low-poly/rough texture/plastic texture/materials without physical properties
- ❌ Cartoon/anime/2D flat style
- ❌ Flat design/no 3D depth/perspective error/lighting logic error
- ❌ Pure Western modern architecture without Chinese core/pure mechanical architecture without Chinese structure/rigid splicing of Chinese and cyber elements
- ❌ Losing Chinese aesthetic core, hollow content only stacking cyber elements
- ❌ Pure Western modern clothing without Chinese design/pure sci-fi prosthetic without Chinese core
- ❌ Vulgar, messy, disorderly visual elements conflicting with Chinese aesthetic tone

> 💡 **Exception**: Modern 3D rendering technology (such as ray tracing, volumetric light, global illumination, physical materials) can be used normally; modern urban architecture with Chinese core, new Chinese clothing, modern scene carriers can all be used normally. The core must adhere to the fusion tone of "Chinese aesthetics as soul, cyber sci-fi as form."
