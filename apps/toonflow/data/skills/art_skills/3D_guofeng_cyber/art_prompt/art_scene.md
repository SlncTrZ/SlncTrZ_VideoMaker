# 3D Guofeng Cyber Scene Image Generation · Constraint Manual

---

## I. Scene Aesthetic Principles

1. **Spatial Narrative** — Scenes carry the emotion and narrative function of Eastern futurism, with traditional Chinese aesthetics as core and cyberpunk technology as extension, compatible with small-scale ancient spatial narratives and large-scale urban Jianghu narratives, never pure background panels
2. **Layered Depth** — All scenes must have three-layer progressive structure of foreground/midground/background; small-scale ancient scenes create depth with foreground cyber devices/holographic projections, midground core guofeng architecture, background landscape clouds; large-scale urban scenes create three-dimensional depth with foreground streets/ancient buildings, midground skyscrapers, background floating cities/electronic clouds; absolutely eliminate flat images
3. **Ancient-Modern Symbiosis** — Ancient and cyber urban elements must organically fuse, retaining Eastern spatial hierarchy, architectural form, aesthetic core; cyber technology serves as upgrade means for guofeng core;eliminate rigid piling and style fragmentation; simultaneously compatible with pure ancient cyberization, pure guofeng cyber city, ancient+urban fusion three scene types
4. **Texture Supreme** — Traditional materials like wood/stone/fabric/water surface and cyber materials like anodized aluminum/carbon fiber/holographic film/neon tubes/tempered glass/electronic screens must have 8K ultra-clear textures, PBR physical-grade texture tangible and distinguishable, adapted to both ancient warm texture and urban industrial texture needs
5. **3D as Anchor** — All images take 3A game-level 3D rendering as absolute standard; reject flat textures, low-quality CG, 2D flat painting textures; enforce pursuit of RTX path tracing, global illumination, volumetric light, ambient occlusion, depth of field blur and other cinematic offline rendering effects; anchor the realistic 3D style of Eastern cyberpunk, simultaneously adapted to ancient soft lighting and urban high-contrast lighting

## V. Four-View Sheet Specification

### View Definition

> Camera fixed at center point of guofeng cyber scene; small-scale ancient scenes use human eye level height; large-scale urban scenes can use high-altitude center point fixation; shoot facing forward/backward/left/right respectively, forming 360° unobstructed panorama, fully presenting scene spatial structure and cyber element layout, compatible with both small-scale ancient space and large-scale urban space.

| Position | View | Viewing Direction | Requirement | Prompt |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Top left | Front view | From center point forward eye level (0°) | Fully display guofeng cyber scene front main structure, front-mid-back depth layers, core cyber devices and Chinese buildings/urban skyline | front view, eye level/high-altitude fixed viewpoint, looking forward, cyberpunk guofeng scene main structure |
| Top right | Right view | From center point right eye level (90°) | Fully display guofeng cyber scene right space extension, building side structure, cyber device layout and spatial depth/urban right skyline | right side view, eye level/high-altitude fixed viewpoint, looking right, cyberpunk guofeng scene side structure |
| Bottom left | Back view | From center point backward eye level (180°) | Fully display guofeng cyber scene back structure, background spatial layers, cyber element back layout and spatial logic/urban back skyline | back view, eye level/high-altitude fixed viewpoint, looking backward, cyberpunk guofeng scene back structure |
| Bottom right | Left view | From center point left eye level (270°) | Fully display guofeng cyber scene left space extension, building side structure, cyber device layout and spatial depth/urban left skyline | left side view, eye level/high-altitude fixed viewpoint, looking left, cyberpunk guofeng scene side structure |

### Frame Specifications

| Item | Constraint |
|---|---|
| Layout | Same frame 2×2 grid evenly arranged: top row (front view + right view), bottom row (back view + left view), forming four-direction complete view from scene center point, no distortion, no cropping |
| People | **Strictly prohibit any characters, human figures, body silhouettes, any living subjects** |
| Viewpoint | All four views depart from same scene center point, sight line height strictly consistent; small-scale scenes use human eye level; large-scale urban scenes use unified high-altitude viewpoint; no height deviation, no distance zoom |
| Consistency | Four views' Chinese architectural structure, cyber device layout, neon light effect positions, holographic projection content, urban skyline, material texture, tone lighting, seasonal weather must be 100% completely consistent, spatial logic self-consistent |
| Lighting | Four views' light source direction strictly unified; natural light + cyber artificial light shadow logic completely consistent; light source position, illumination intensity, shadow direction under different viewing angles fully conform to physical rules |
| Aspect ratio | Enforce 1:1 square frame (adapted to 2×2 grid even arrangement) |
