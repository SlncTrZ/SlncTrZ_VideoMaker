## I. Derivative Principles
(Core Constraints: Ancient/Urban Dual-Scene Universal, All Variants Strictly Follow "Style Fusion Unified, Spatial Logic Consistent")

1. **Spatial Consistency** — Building structure/layout/materials, guofeng cyber core fusion elements remain completely consistent across all variants
 - Ancient scenes: Fixed ancient building form/flying eaves brackets/sunmao structure/courtyard layout/traditional patterns cyber renovation logic
 - Urban scenes: Fixed building structure/street network/urban functional area layout/guofeng renovation elements (Chinese flying eaves/brackets/patterns) fusion logic
 - Element misalignment, structural changes, style fragmentation between variants strictly prohibited
2. **Shot-Driven** — Same scene through different shotsdisplaying different narrative functions; precisely adapted to ancient/urban scene spatial narrative logic
3. **Time Period Switch** — Same space presents different light and shadow atmospheres in different time periods; simultaneously adapt dedicated light source brightness, color temperature and on/off logic for dual scenes
 - Ancient scenes: Traditional lanterns/rune lamps/holographic guofeng projections/neon pipelines
 - Urban scenes: Building large screens/streetlights/car lights/guofeng neon signs/holographic ink advertisements
4. **Weather Change** — Same space presents different emotions under different weather; simultaneously adapt material and element physical feedback for dual scenes
 - Ancient scenes: Rain-mist neon Tyndall, snow covering flying eaves, dripping eaves tiles, wet wood texture
 - Urban scenes: Glass curtain wall rain mark reflection, road puddled water neon reflection, snow covering building flying eaves, metal structure frost
5. **3D as Anchor** — All variants must maintain 3D rendering quality; reject flat textures/CG animation/2D flat painting style; strictly retain PBR physical material, ray tracing global illumination, volumetric light, ambient occlusion, depth of field blur; strengthen dual-scene dedicated material texture
 - Ancient exclusive: Weathered wood structure, aged metal, brick and tile texture, fabric drape, paint wear, moss weathering
 - Urban exclusive: Ultra-white glass curtain wall, anodized aluminum material, fair-faced concrete, asphalt pavement, matte metal, LED self-luminous screen
