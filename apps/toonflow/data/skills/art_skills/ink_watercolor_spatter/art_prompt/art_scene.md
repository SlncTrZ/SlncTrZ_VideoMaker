# Scene Background Generation · Ink and Watercolor Spatter Constraint Manual

---

## I. Scene Principles

1. **Ink Wash Landscape Aesthetics** — Scene built from watercolor washes, wet-on-wet blooms, dry brush texture, and sumi-e line accents
2. **Atmospheric Perspective through Wash Intensity** — Foreground = bold C1 + saturated wash; Midground = moderate wash; Background = faint C8 pale wash veil
3. **Negative Space as Composition** — Unpainted rice paper (C2) carries equal weight to painted areas
4. **Suggestion over Depiction** — Mountains as wash shapes, trees as ink dots, water as unpainted paper
5. **Texture as Mood** — Spatter for turbulence, wet bloom for dreaminess, dry brush for aged stillness

## IV. Composition Principles

1. **Asymmetry** — Subject offset from center, inspired by Chinese ink painting (S-curve, diagonal flow)
2. **Flowing Lines** — Wash and brush strokes guide the eye through the scene
3. **Negative Space Emphasis** — 30-50% of frame reserved as unpainted C2 rice paper
4. **Foreground/Midground/Background** — Three layers of wash intensity separate depth
5. **Ink Accent Points** — Small C1 or C3 concentrated marks (birds, figures, seals) for focal contrast

## VI. Constraint Rules

### Mandatory

| Number | Rule |
|---|---|
| R1 | Scene must have "foreground/midground/background" defined by wash intensity |
| R2 | Must include "atmospheric perspective through wash intensity" |
| R3 | Scene must be "single frame" — no multi-view collage |
| R4 | **No people allowed** in scene images |
| R5 | Must include "ink and watercolor spatter" style keywords |
| R6 | Composition must use ink painting asymmetry — no centered symmetry |
| R7 | 30-50% of frame must be negative space (C2 unpainted rice paper) |

### Prohibited

| Number | Prohibition |
|---|---|
| X1 | Photorealism or 3D rendered landscape |
| X2 | Symmetrical centered composition |
| X3 | Fully painted frame with no negative space |
| X4 | Hard digital gradients — watercolor bloom only |
| X5 | Any people, human figures, or silhouettes |
| X6 | Neon or digital color palette — use C1-C10 ink-wash palette only |
| X7 | Scene without wash depth — must have atmospheric fade |
