# Ink and Watercolor Spatter · Neo-Traditional Ink-Wash Style

This style is specifically crafted for the "Ink and Watercolor Spatter" theme — a neo-traditional fusion where East Asian ink wash painting (sumi-e, 水墨) meets free-flowing watercolor spatter. All art prompts, specifications, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Ink Wash Foundation**: Black ink (#1A1A1A) as the soul of every piece — deliberate brush strokes, controlled bleeding, whispered outlines
- **Watercolor Emotion**: Free-flowing watercolor blooms that set the emotional tone — vermillion passion, indigo melancholy, jade serenity
- **Rice Paper Texture**: Warm white canvas (#F5F0E8) visible in negative space, subtle grain, pigment soaking into fiber
- **Controlled Chaos**: Accidental splashes coexist with intentional strokes; the painting breathes like it is still drying

## Core Philosophy

- **Ink is the bone** — structure, line, shadow, permanence. Every piece begins with ink.
- **Watercolor is the breath** — color blooms, washes, gradients of feeling. Color is emotion made visible.
- **Chaos is welcome** — spatter, drip, bleed, bloom. Imperfection is authenticity.
- **Empty space is voice** — silence between strokes speaks as loudly as the ink itself.

## Scope

- AI art generation for ink-wash watercolor fusion themes (characters, scenes, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiring poetic brushwork, pigment texture, and expressive color washes
- Neo-traditional ink art, literary illustration, atmospheric concept art, expressive portraiture

## Prohibited Content

- 3D rendering, photorealism, CG effects, digital painting look
- Neon colors, high-saturation fluorescent, harsh digital gradients
- Perfect symmetry or mechanical precision (ink art celebrates imperfection)
- Excessive detail, photo-level texture, hyperrealistic rendering
- Western oil painting style, thick impasto, acrylic-heavy texture

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Characters emerge from ink washes with watercolor accents — faces suggested rather than fully rendered, hair as flowing ink strokes
- Scenes built on negative space — misty mountains, drifting petals, rain-slicked streets dissolving into pigment blooms
- Storyboards read like a scroll painting unfurling — ink spatter at emotional peaks, watercolor bleeding across panel boundaries

---

This style is only applicable to projects related to "Ink and Watercolor Spatter · Neo-Traditional Ink-Wash." For other styles (such as realistic 3D, modern anime, flat design, etc.), do not use this set of prompts.
