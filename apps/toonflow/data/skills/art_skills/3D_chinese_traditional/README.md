# Chinese Traditional 3D Style Guide

This style is crafted for "Chinese Traditional 3D" themes. All art prompts, guidelines, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Chinese Traditional 3D Rendering**: Uses 3D modeling and rendering techniques to present traditional Chinese aesthetics, covering ancient architecture, landscape gardens, court costumes, etc.
- **3D Fine Texture**: High-precision models, PBR material rendering, rich light-shadow layering, clear and discernible details
- **Chinese Traditional Color Palette**: Traditional colors such as blue-green, vermillion, indigo, golden yellow; harmonious and elegant coloring
- **Eastern Aesthetic Atmosphere**: Profound artistic conception, interplay ofvirtual and real, negative space composition, poetic space
- **Ancient Character Styling**: 3D ancient modeling, ornate elegant costumes, refined makeup, meticulous hairstyles
- **Cinematic Lighting**: Volumetric light, ambient occlusion, global illumination, depth-of-field blur and other cinematic rendering effects

## Scope

- AI art generation for Chinese Traditional 3D themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiring 3D renderingtexture, Eastern aesthetics, ancient artistic conception
- Application scenarios such as game art, film concept design, traditional Chinese animation

## Prohibited Content

- Photorealistic/photography-level realism (unless otherwise specified)
- Western fantasy/Gothic/Victorian style
- Cyberpunk/sci-fi/modern urban elements
- Low-precision models/rough modeling/blurry textures
- Oversaturated/fluorescent/digital-heavy feel

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Elegant and refined characterstyling, delicate 3D modeling, clear costume textures, flowing hair strands
- Grand scenes with rich architectural details, realistic materialtexture, poetic light and shadow atmosphere
- Exquisite props and artifacts with fine craftsmanship, harmonious and unified colors
- Storyboards and rendering emphasize 3Dtexture, Eastern artistic conception, cinematic lighting
