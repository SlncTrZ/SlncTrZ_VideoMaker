# Mature Urban Romance Anime Style Guide

This style is crafted for **mature urban romance anime** themes. All art prompts, guidelines, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **Anime style**: All characters, scenes, and props follow anime standards, emphasizing cel shading, clean lines, and dramatic low-key lighting.
- **Modern urban romance setting**: Modern urban life scenes including apartments, offices, cafes, city streets, etc. No traditional/fantasy elements.
- **Cinematic composition**: Frame composition emphasizes a cinematic feel with depth-of-field blur, lens optical characteristics, and atmospheric perspective.
- **Low-saturation cool tones**: Overall color palette leans cool, with warm colors used only as accents, highlighting a sweet and tender atmosphere where cool elegance meets warmth.

## Scope

- AI art generation for mature urban romance themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation for modern novel-adapted animation styles
- Visual work requiring clean lines, cel shading, and cinematic composition

## Prohibited Content

- Photorealistic/real-photography/3D rendering styles
- High-saturation fluorescent/neon colors
- Styles outside modern elements such as traditional/fantasy/sci-fi
- Suggestive, revealing, or inappropriate content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Characters with refined features, cool or gentle temperament; clothing, makeup, and hairstyles in modern urban anime style
- Rich spatial layering in scenes, ultra-clear material textures, poetic and realistic atmosphere
- Props, decorations, and accessories in modern urban styling with fine craftsmanship andextreme/ultimate quality
- Storyboards and videos based on anime, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style applies only to "mature urban romance anime" related projects. For other styles (such as photorealistic, traditional, fantasy, etc.), do not use this prompt set.
