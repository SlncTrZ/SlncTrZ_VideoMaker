# Urban Romance Constraints · Mature Urban Romance Anime · Technique Reference

---

## I. Color System and Visual Tone

- **Color base** — The entire film uses cool white skin (C1), light blue (C3), cyan-gray (C6) as base colors. Overall color temperature leans cool (5800-7000K), saturation mid-low (30-50%), presenting a cool restrained high-end gray tone
- **Cool/warm narrative contrast** — Warm colors (amber warm C7, smoke pink C5) serve as visual signals for narrativeturn/twist, used forpartialaccents in emotional returnwarm andambiguous risingwarm segments. Cool/warmswitching should synchronize with the story arc, notcasual mixing
- **Cool/warm allocation strategy** — Separation/suppression/detachment segments strictly maintain cool tone base (cyan-gray C6 + light blue C3),prohibited amber warm, smoke pink and other warm tones; warm colors released only after emotional returnwarm, using cool/warm contrast as narrativeturn/twist
- **Palette-first principle** — Paragraph planning must first bind emotional scenes (first sight/heart flutter,ambiguous risingwarm,protectcommitment, separation/misunderstanding, reunion/release, night banquet/lantern festival, etc.), then determine main color + secondary color and lighting scheme, avoiding "story arc is right but emotion color is wrong"
- **Prohibited color gamut** — High-saturation fluorescent colors, neon colors, modern digital color systems are all incompatible with this style

## II. Lighting Scheme System

- **Lighting is narrative** — 7 lighting schemes correspond to different emotional segments. The director shoulddetermine/confirm lighting tone direction at the paragraph level during planning, rather than specifying per shot
- **Cel animation lighting characteristics** — Clear light-dark boundary lines, dramatic low-key lighting as coremeans; lighting must serve emotional narrative, maintaining the cel shading style of anime

| Lighting Scheme | Scheme Name | Huetendency | Applicable Emotions |
|---|---|---|---|
| A | Soft side-backlight | Cool white base +partial warm lightoutline tracing | Heart flutter, tenderness, daily warmth |
| B | Hard side high contrast | Ink black C4 + silver gray C8 hard lightdivision | Confrontation pressure, anger coldness, tension conflict |
| C | Diffused cool light | Cyan-gray C6 + light blue C3 low-key lighting | Suppression sadness, separation misunderstanding, loneliness |
| D | Diffused warm light | Amber warm C7 + smoke pink C5partial | Ambiguityrising warm, reunion release, intimacy sweetness |
| E | Window natural light | Pure white C10 + light blue C3 sidemottled light | Indoor daytime, daily life, workplace commute |
| F | Night scene lights | Amber warm C7accents + cyan-gray C6shadow area | Night banquet lanterns, city night view, romantic flowing light |
| G | Moonlight cool blue | Light blue C3 + silver gray C8 cool light | Night loneliness, yearning solitude, cold detachment |

- **Cool/warm light allocation** — Warm light (amber warm C7) suitable used for emotional returnwarm,ambiguous intimacy segments; cool light (cyan-gray C6 + light blue C3) suitable used for separation, suppression, detachment segments. Director can adjust cool/warm switching points based on narrative needs
- **Atmosphere direction mapping** — The atmosphere direction of each scene should map to one of the above lighting schemes (A-G), ensuring visual consistency

## III. Texture Direction

- **Cel animation as anchor** — Core of mature urban romance anime: clean lines, cel shading, dramatic low-key lighting, presenting cinematic anime visuals
- **Face and skin** — Delicate facial rendering, delicate skin, refined features; female cool white skin (C1) translucent not pallid, male warm white skin (C2) fresh and natural. Skin maintains smooth delicate cel shadingtexture
- **Costume materials** — Clear fabric texture, ultra-clear texture details, realistic fabric folds, fabric flowing with movement. Fabrictexture of modern urban clothing (suit/shirt/dress/coat) must be believable
- **Scene materials** — Clear scene material textures, atmospheric perspective, modern weartraces, lived-in feel. Glass reflection, metal gloss, wood texture, fabric drape must all be clearly presented under cel animation style
- **Cel animation is not flat** — Mature urban romance anime emphasizes superimposing cinematic lighting and depth-of-field blur on the basis of cel shading, amplifying emotional impact through composition and emotion design, rather than relying on realistic accumulation

## IV. Urban Scene Space Elements

Unique scene elements of modern urban worldview and their visual narrative functions:

- **Floor-to-ceiling windows/glass partitions/door frames** — Natural frame composition props, creating "unfathomable" layering and spatial depth. Glass reflection and transmission are visual highlights
- **City skyline/streets/park** — Natural carriers of negative space composition; scene is emotion: bustling streets = loneliness amid noise, sitting alone on a bench in the rain = solitude, fallen leaves drifting across the street = parting sorrow
- **Lamps/window light/screen light** — Light carriers of modern urban scenes, indoor warm lamp = warmth/intimacy (Scheme D), city night view = cool-warminterwoven (Scheme F), natural window light = daily/peaceful (Scheme E)
- **Use empty scene shots between paragraphs for transition** — This style has rich scene assets (different time periods/weather variants). Scene transitions should use empty scene shots as emotional buffers, not hard cuts. City skyline, rainy window view, empty cafe can all serve as transition empty shots
- **Use visuals rather than dialogue at turning points** — Prioritize visual means (lightingsudden change, shot sizejump cut, empty shot metaphor) rather than relying on dialogue explanation

## V. Urban Music and Ambient Sound

Sound element constraints under the modern urban romance worldview:

### Musical Instrument Selection

- **Piano** — Core instrument, widest applicability: soloused for solitude/yearning/memory segments, light touchused for daily/heart-flutter segments
- **Strings (violin/cello/string ensemble)** — Emotional foundation andsublimation, cello low tones suitable for suppression/restraint, violin high notes suitable for heart flutter/throb, string ensemble suitable for emotional climax
- **Guitar (acoustic/electric)** — Acoustic guitarused for daily warmth/relaxed segments, electric guitar clean toneused for urban night scene/buzzed atmosphere
- **Electronic ambient sounds** — Low-frequency electronic padused for city empty shot/loneliness segments as atmosphere foundation, adding modern urban spatial feel
- **Flute/clarinet** — Light segmentaccents, not dominant,used for morning/park/daily atmosphere supplement

### Music Combination Strategy

| Emotional Stage | Instrument Combination |
|---|---|
| Calm/Opening/Closing | Piano solo or Piano + light strings |
| Heart flutter/Throb | Piano + Violin |
| Ambiguityrising warm/Intimacy | Acoustic guitar + light piano + strings foundation |
| Suppression/Sadness/Separation | Cello solo or Cello + Piano |
| Emotional outburst/Reunion/Climax | Full string ensemble + Piano |
| Urban night scene/Loneliness | Electronic ambient + Piano |
| Daily warmth/Leisure | Acoustic guitar + Flute + light percussion |

### Urban Ambient Sound

- **Typical ambient sound layers** — Distant traffic flow / Rain on window glass / Coffee machine running / Keyboard typing / High heels clicking / Elevator chime / City night wind / Distant car horns / Park birdsong / Restaurant slight bustle
- **Mark 1-2 core ambient sounds per scene** to assist subsequent sound design. The richer the ambient sound layers, the more immersive the urban scene
