# Storyboard Urban Romance Constraints · Mature Urban Romance Anime · Technique Reference

---

## I. Storyboard Positioning

The storyboard is the director's core tool for translatingscript intolens/shotlanguage. The following provides only the constraint norms for the mature urban romance anime style at the storyboard level.

---

## II. Light and Atmosphere

- **Unified lighting within the same scene** — No more than two lighting schemes should appear within a single scene, unless there is a clear narrativeturn/twist (e.g., indoor light switch → deepening dusk outside the window, sunny → sudden drizzle)
- **Emotion palette binding** — Each scene should bind at least 1 emotional scenario (first sight/heart flutter,ambiguous risingwarm,protectcommitment, separation/misunderstanding, reunion/release, night banquet/lantern festival), and maintain main color + secondary color continuity within the shot group
- **Light source angle norms** — Default camera-facinglens/shot light source uses soft side-backlight or diffused light; side-facinglens/shot adjustments based onimage needs. When describing light, distinguish between natural light (window light/sunlight) and artificial light (lamp light/screen light)
- **Cool/warm tone matching narrative stage** — Separation/suppression/detachment segments strictly use cool-colored light (cyan-gray C6 + light blue C3),prohibited amber warm/smoke pink warm tones; warm tones (amber warm C7 + smoke pink C5) used only in emotional returnwarm,ambiguous risingwarm segments
- **Lighting transitions are advanced technique** — Gradual change from natural window light to indoor warm lamp light = day-to-night time passage; bright office to dim apartment = emotionalturn/twist. Mark lighting change points in the storyboard

---

## III. Environmental Dynamics

- **Environmental dynamics add breathing room to the frame** — Curtains gently swaying, coffee cup steam rising, raindrops sliding down glass, city lights flickering, leaves falling. Arrange at least one shot with environmental dynamics every 3-4 shots to avoid the frame feeling "dead"
- **Modern urban environment elementspriority** — Environmental dynamics should use elements within the modern urbanworld view: curtains gently swaying, car light shadows, rain streaks across glass, fallen leaves drifting across the street, coffee steam rising, street lamp halos flickering. Prohibit traditional/fantasy elements
- **Cel animation dynamic expression** — Environmental dynamics should conform to anime expression methods: hand-paintedtexture of dappled light and shadow, line beauty of fabric movement, celrendering effects of rain streaks and lights

---

## IV. Urban Character Action Rhythm

- **Restrained actions** — Mature urban romance character movements should be restrained andimplicit, emotions not outwardly displayed. Key actions (turning, raising a hand, looking back) should be marked "slow" or "composed"
- **Costume dynamics** — Micro-dynamics of modern urban clothing (suit hem, skirt hem, scarf, coat hem) are natural visual assets. Include slight fabric movement with wind/action in scene descriptions to add a sense of "life"
- **Posture constraints** — Urban romance character demeanor should match mature elitetemperament: standing posture tall and composed, movements elegant and restrained, avoiding exaggerated anime body language (such as exaggerated gestures, chibideformation expressions)
- **Micro-expressionspriority** — Use facial micro-expressions (mouth cornerarc, eyebrow slight movement, eye gaze shift) to convey emotions, rather than relying on large limb movements

---

## V. Camera Movement Taboos

- **Fast camera movementprohibited** — Whip pans, quick pushes, handheld shaking conflict with the cool eleganttemperament of mature urban romance anime
- **Fancy transitionsprohibited** — Wipe, rotate, blinds, flash white, etc. are incompatible with this style
- **Use depth of field andspace** — Utilize the spatial depth of modern urban scenes for slow push/pull or tracking shots. Create a cinematic feel through shallow depth-of-field blur, but maintain a steady and restrained rhythm
