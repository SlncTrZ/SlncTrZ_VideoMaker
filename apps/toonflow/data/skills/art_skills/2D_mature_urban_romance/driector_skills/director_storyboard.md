## IV. Fixed Style Anchor Words (Must be included in all outputs)

**Anime style anchor (mandatory):**

Anime style, cel shading, clean lines, cinematic composition, dramatic low-key lighting,

**Charactertexture (mandatory when character shots are included):**

Delicate facial rendering, delicate skin, refined features, layered hair strands, delicate hair rendering

**Costumetexture (mandatory when character shots are included):**

Clear fabric texture, ultra-clear texture details, realistic fabric folds, fabric flowing with movement

**Scenetexture (mandatory when scene shots are included):**

Clear scene material textures, atmospheric perspective, modern weartraces, lived-in feel, depth-of-field blur

**Consistency anchor (mandatory for reference image mode):**

Keep character face consistent with reference image, keep costume colors consistent with reference image, keep scene lighting styleunified

**Style closing (fixed):**

Mature urban romance anime style, modern novel adaptation animation, cinematic storyboard composition

**Quality lock words (must be included in all outputs, placed after style closing):**

### Mode A (Chinese)

Default (when no in-frame text is needed):
Ultra-clear 4K quality, high detail, natural sharpness, realistic clarity, no subtitles, no watermark, no title overlay

In-frame textscene (when scene description contains prop text like writing/scrolls/plaques):
Ultra-clear 4K quality, high detail, natural sharpness, realistic clarity, no subtitles, no watermark, no title overlay, text on scrolls/plaques and other scene props clearly legible

### Mode B (English)

Default:
ultra-sharp 4K, high detail, crisp textures, naturalistic sharpness, photorealistic clarity, no subtitles, no captions, no watermark, no title overlay

In-frame textscene:
ultra-sharp 4K, high detail, crisp textures, naturalistic sharpness, photorealistic clarity, no subtitles, no captions, no watermark, no title overlay, legible text on in-scene props such as scrolls and plaques

**Negative prompt template (Mode B must include, placed at end of prompt):**

> ⚠️ Seedream (Mode A) **does not support negative prompts**. Negative prompts only apply to Mode B. Mode A ensures quality through texture anchoring and quality locking in positive prompts.

Mode B (English):
no plastic skin, no beauty filter, no studio lighting, no centered composition, no oversaturation, no AI generated look, no motion blur, no noise, no blurry, no out of focus, no subtitles, no captions, no watermark, no title overlay, no UI text

## V. Aesthetic Prohibitions (Strictly avoid during generation)

The following words/styles must not appear in output prompts:

- ❌ Photorealistic/real-photography/3D rendering related words (e.g., photorealistic, real photography, CGI)
- ❌ Styles outside modern elements such as traditional/fantasy/sci-fi
- ❌ High-saturation fluorescent/neon colors
- ❌ Cartoon proportions, big eyes, chibi and otherdeformation descriptions (unless specific style requirements)
- ❌ Moderntech elements in frame (phone screens, computer interfaces, modern signage, etc.)
- ❌ Overlaid on-screen text (subtitles, watermarks, title cards, narrationoverlay text, opening titles and other UI layer text; the frame must be purely visual)

> 💡 **Exception**: Prop text within the story world (characters writing, scroll inscriptions, plaques, signboards, letters, prescriptions and other naturally occurring text in the scene) **is not within the prohibited range**. When the storyboard scene description includes such content, describe its existence truthfully and require the text to be clear.
