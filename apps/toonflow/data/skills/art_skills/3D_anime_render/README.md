# 3D Anime Render Style Guide

This style is crafted for "3D anime render" themes. All art prompts, guidelines, and generated content are strictly limited to:

- **3D anime worldview**: Set in a modern urban background, covering streets, cafes, homes, offices, shopping malls, etc., reflecting the atmosphere of modern urban life.
- **Cel shadingtexture**: All characters, scenes, and props follow 3D anime render standards, emphasizing clear outline lines, materialfine/delicate degree, and light-shadow layering.
- **Healing aesthetic**: Pursues a cinematic feel on the basis of 3D rendering, with warm and brightimagetone/color tone, rich details, and an overall warm, healing, and lively atmosphere.
- **Emotional expression**: Suitable for showcasing gentle, healing, romantic urban love stories, emphasizing emotional expression under the modern urban aesthetic.

## Scope

- AI art generation for 3D anime render themes (characters, scenes, props, storyboards, etc.)
- Visual creation requiring ultimate 3D renderingtexture, delicate materials, and a warm healing atmosphere

## Prohibited Content

- Realistic rendering, photorealistic realism
- Dark tones, heavy shadows, excessive contrast
- High-saturation fluorescent colors, neon colors
- Lack of modern elements, dark violence, adult-oriented content

## Style Experience

Under this style, you will experience:

- Characters with refined features, gentle or bright temperament; clothing, makeup, and hairstyles in 3D anime render style
- Rich spatial layering in scenes, clear and smooth outline lines, poetic and realistic atmosphere
- Props, daily items, and environmental details in modern styling with fine craftsmanship andfine/delicatetexture
- Storyboards and visuals based on 3D rendering, emphasizing narrative and emotional expression

---

This style applies only to "3D anime render" related projects. For other styles (such as realistic 3D, CG, illustration, modern, etc.), do not use this prompt set.
